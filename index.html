Tank Tips

  1. Change water and clean gravel
  2. Rinse filter often
  3. Don't overfeed
  4. Keep tank out of direct sunlight

Diving & Harvesting Tips

Diving Tips

  1. Never dive without a buddy
  2. Never dive if you have a cold
  3. Always plan your dive, and always dive your plan
  4. Check your diving equipment to make sure it works
  5. Do not drink alcohol or take drugs before diving

Harvesting Tips

  1. Hand Line
  2. Pot & Trap/Creel
  3. Diver Foraged
